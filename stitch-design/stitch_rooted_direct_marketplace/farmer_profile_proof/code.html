Our Story

Nestled in the rolling hills, Willow Creek Farm has been committed to regenerative agriculture since long before it was a trend. We believe that healthy soil creates healthy plants, which in turn nourish healthy communities. Our approach is hands-on, patient, and deeply respectful of the natural rhythms of the seasons.

Location Portland, Oregon
Established 1978
