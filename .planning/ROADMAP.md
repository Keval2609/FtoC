### Phase Details

**Phase 1: Marketplace & Profiles MVP**
Goal: Launch the core consumer buying experience with farmer storytelling profiles and verification.
Requirements: DISCO-01, PROF-01, VERIF-01, BUY-01
Success criteria:
1. Consumer can view a list of farms and their verified certificates.
2. Farmer can create a profile with story and product listings.
3. Consumer can complete a checkout flow directly linking to a farmer.

**Phase 2: Communication & Community**
Goal: Add direct messaging and community features.
Requirements: MSG-01, COMM-01, MONET-01
Success criteria:
1. Consumer can securely message a farmer.
2. Platform processes a transaction fee cut.
