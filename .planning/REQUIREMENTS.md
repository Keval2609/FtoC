## v1 Requirements

### Discovery & Purchasing
- [ ] **DISCO-01**: Consumer can view and filter a directory of natural farmers.
- [ ] **BUY-01**: Consumer can add produce to cart and checkout.

### Profiles & Trust
- [ ] **PROF-01**: Farmers can create profiles detailing their story, methods, and photos.
- [ ] **VERIF-01**: Farmers can upload organic/natural certifications visible to consumers.

### Platform Expansion (Phase 2)
- [ ] **MSG-01**: Consumers and farmers can send direct messages.
- [ ] **COMM-01**: Users can access a community feed for recipes and events.
- [ ] **MONET-01**: The platform can charge fees on transactions.
